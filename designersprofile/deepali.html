About the Designer

Deepali Bhole is the founder and creative force behind KDUJ, a clothing brand born from her passion to create special, comfortable outfits for children. An ex-IT professional with six years of experience, Deepali transformed her desire to design stylish clothes into a thriving brand named after her family’s initials.

KDUJ reflects her creativity, warmth, and the joy of motherhood, with designs that emphasize individuality, comfort, and style. ModaSou is excited to collaborate with Deepali to offer beautifully crafted, customized clothing with a personal touch in every stitch.
