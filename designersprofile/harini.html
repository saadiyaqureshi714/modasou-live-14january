About the Designer

Harini Ravisankar is a skilled fashion designer with over 5 years of experience in the fashion industry. Currently owning a fashion designing company called Annkh, she has honed her expertise in fashion illustration, styling, and over 15 related skills.

Her professional journey includes significant roles such as Graphic Designer at Gurpzit, where she specialized in printing and graphics, and internships at prestigious organizations like Reliance Retail and Naidu Hall, focusing on woven and knitwear design.

A graduate of the National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) with a Bachelor’s degree in Knitwear Design, Harini’s strong educational foundation and industry experience make her an invaluable asset to ModaSou.
