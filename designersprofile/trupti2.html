About the Designer

Trupti’s passion for fashion design began over a decade ago, evolving from showcasing her work at exhibitions to creating bespoke designs tailored to individual client needs. For the past eight years, she has specialized in custom stitching, bringing her clients’ visions to life with precision and creativity.

Known for her seamless craftsmanship and affordable, unique styles, Trupti blends traditional techniques with modern trends, ensuring each piece reflects the client’s personality. Her dedication to quality and attention to detail make her a standout addition to ModaSou.
